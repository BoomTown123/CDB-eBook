# Communities

> Communities for AI practitioners building AI-first companies. Developer communities, professional networks, conferences, and open source communities where the topics in the book are actively discussed.

---

## Developer Communities

Online communities where AI engineers, researchers, and builders share knowledge and collaborate.

| Community | Platform | Focus | URL |
|-----------|----------|-------|-----|
| **Hugging Face** | Web + Discord | The central hub for open source AI -- model sharing, datasets, Spaces for demos, and active discussion forums. | https://huggingface.co |
| **r/MachineLearning** | Reddit | Research-focused discussion of ML papers, techniques, and industry developments. 3M+ members. | https://reddit.com/r/MachineLearning |
| **r/LocalLLaMA** | Reddit | Focused on running open-weight models locally -- Llama, Mistral, quantization, and self-hosting. Relevant to the open-source model discussion in Chapter 3. | https://reddit.com/r/LocalLLaMA |
| **r/artificial** | Reddit | Broader AI discussion covering industry news, ethics, and applications. | https://reddit.com/r/artificial |
| **LangChain Discord** | Discord | LangChain and LangSmith builders collaborate on LLM applications with chains and agents. | https://discord.gg/langchain |
| **LlamaIndex Discord** | Discord | LlamaIndex users building RAG systems and data-connected LLM applications. | https://discord.gg/llamaindex |
| **Weights & Biases Community** | Discord + Forums | ML practitioners sharing experiment tracking, model evaluation, and production ML practices. | https://wandb.ai/community |
| **MLOps Community** | Slack + Web | Practitioners focused on ML operations, deployment, and production systems. | https://mlops.community |

## Getting Started Recommendations

### If you are a technical practitioner:
1. Join the **Hugging Face** community and explore model repos
2. Join the **LangChain** or **LlamaIndex** Discord based on your stack
3. Follow **r/LocalLLaMA** for open-weight model developments
4. Attend the **AI Engineer Summit** for production AI engineering

### If you are a business leader:
1. Subscribe to the newsletters listed in [Courses & Learning](courses-and-learning.md)
2. Join **AI Tinkerers** in your city for hands-on exposure
3. Attend **NVIDIA GTC** or **AWS re:Invent** for enterprise AI perspectives
4. Connect with the **Chief AI Officer Network** on LinkedIn

### If you are building a team:
1. Start with the [90-Day AI Fluency Program](../frameworks/90-day-ai-fluency-program.md)
2. Use **MLOps Community** Slack for production ML questions
3. Use **Weights & Biases** community for experiment tracking practices
4. Attend **Ray Summit** for scaling AI infrastructure
