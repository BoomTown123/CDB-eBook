## Tools & Platforms

- [Cursor](https://www.cursor.com/) -- AI-native code editor that reached $100M ARR in roughly one year, built by a small team.
- [GitHub Copilot](https://github.com/features/copilot) -- AI pair programmer; GitHub's study found developers complete tasks 55% faster with Copilot.
- [Replit Agent](https://replit.com/) -- AI development platform capable of producing three production apps in under 200 minutes for less than $25.
- [Vercel v0](https://v0.dev/) -- AI-powered UI generation tool that creates functional MVPs in 15 minutes from simple prompts.
- [DeepSeek](https://www.deepseek.com/) -- Open-weight model provider offering comparable capability to GPT-4 at $0.28 per million tokens.
- [Fireworks AI](https://fireworks.ai/) -- Model serving and fine-tuning platform; helped Notion reduce AI latency from 2 seconds to 350 milliseconds.
